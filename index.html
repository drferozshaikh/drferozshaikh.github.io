Professional Role

This service explains why a seafarer medical result occurs and what realistic outcome can be expected on repeat examination.
It does not replace the examining doctor. It prepares you before attending one.
Focus: interpretation of maritime medical standards, operational risk reasoning and outcome probability.

Why Different Doctors Give Different Results

Fitness at sea depends on risk during isolation, not only diagnosis.
Medication stability
Emergency management possibility onboard
Watchkeeping and duty safety impact
Voyage duration and evacuation access
Flag state and company tolerance thresholds

Submission Definition

Scope & Limitations

This is an interpretation service, not a certification authority.
No medical certificate issued
No treatment advice provided
No guarantee of employment decision
