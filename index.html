How Medical Fitness Decisions Are Interpreted

Fitness for sea duty is not based on diagnosis alone. Operational role, watchkeeping duties, medication stability, voyage duration and repatriation risk are assessed together.
Two doctors may legally reach different decisions depending on operational interpretation — this service explains the reasoning behind such outcomes.

Common Questions

Declared UNFIT — what does it actually mean?

It does not always end a career. Often it reflects risk prediction for specific vessel conditions rather than permanent inability to work.

Different clinics giving different results?

Guidelines allow interpretation. Vessel type, flag rules and job role influence decision thresholds.

Can I join after surgery?

Return depends on recovery stability, recurrence risk and emergency management feasibility at sea.
